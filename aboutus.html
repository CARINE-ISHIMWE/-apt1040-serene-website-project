Serene Stay is a worldclass resort, We offer world class services.
Serene Stay in Limuru, Kenya is the perfect location to go to unwind and relax. We strive to give a perfect environment and atmosphere to our guests. Lots and lots of Team building activies are available, 8 Deluxe bedrooms (double/twin), 5 Family Suites, and the Volcano Manor(a honeymoon and romantic villa) are modernly fitted to allow for your stay. Also, for the food lovers we have 3 restaurants that offer varying cusine. We have a team of world-class chefs who deliver everything from gourmet to simplistic meals tailored to fit your needs.
